Site de Lamastrock

D’abord organisatrice du festival Lamastre en Scène qui a rassemblé plus de 50 000 spectateurs sur 7 éditions de 1998 à 2004, Lamastrock a ensuite initié de très nombreux projets de diffusion, saisons de concerts, actions culturelles et autres festivals (notamment Rockmovies, Terr’a Tempos).

Lamastrock porte aujourd’hui plus de 300 concerts par an en France et à l’étranger.

At first organizer of Lamastre en Scene festival which attracted over 50,000 spectators over 7 editions from 1998 to 2004, Lamastrock then initiated a large number of projects, concert seasons, festivals and other cultural activities (including Rockmovies, Terr'A Tempo).

Lamastrock produces over 300 concerts per year in France and abroad.

Note: I didn't need to use english in this project, except for resolving issues on forums.

The Lamastrock site in Wordpress was not responsive design and could not be upgraded without breaking dependencies, it was necessary to rebuild it.

Application Plannings

Cinéma associatif, et circuit itinérant en milieu rural, classé Art et Essai et labellisé Jeune Public. Écran Village fonctionne avec une équipe d’une trentaine de bénévoles, issue de ces différentes communes d’accueil. Cette équipe est appuyée par deux salariés, permettant à l’association de développer son offre auprès des différents publics, de renforcer son action dans le domaine de l’éducation à l’image et d’avoir une gestion professionnelle de ses activités.

Associative cinema, and traveling circuit in rural areas classified Art et Essais and labeled for Young Audiences, Ecran Village works with a team of thirty volunteers, from these various villages. This team is supported by two employees, enabling the association to expand its offering to different audiences, to strengthen its action in the field of image education and have a professional management of its activities.

This application has been developed for a mobile cinema to organise the voluntary workers for the projectionnists and the cashier for each show. Since we've got numeric projectors, the activity has consequently increased and we needed a tool to make the planning tasks easier. Actually, the application is in production here on a Heroku's server because it's a simple way to deploy a Ruby on Rails application. As it's also deployed on another server for the management of our Plannings.
